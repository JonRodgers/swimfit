# SWIMFIT 2026 Financial Plan

## Executive Summary

SWIMFIT's financial model demonstrates strong profitability and scalability, with projected 2026 revenue of HK$5.2M and EBITDA of HK$1.3M (25% margin). The business has achieved profitability in 2024 and is on a clear trajectory to market leadership in Hong Kong's premium swimming education segment.

**Key Financial Highlights (2026)**:
- **Revenue**: HK$5.2M (73% growth from 2025)
- **Gross Profit**: HK$1.8M (35% gross margin)
- **EBITDA**: HK$1.3M (25% EBITDA margin)
- **Net Income**: HK$900K (17% net margin)
- **Customer Base**: 800-1,000 active students
- **Revenue per Student**: HK$5,200-6,500 annually
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: HK$1,500-2,000
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: HK$15,000-25,000
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: 8-12:1 (excellent unit economics)

**Funding Requirements**: HK$500K-800K for 2026 expansion (facility setup, marketing, technology, working capital)

## Financial Risks & Mitigation

### Key Financial Risks

**1. Revenue Concentration**
- **Risk**: Over-reliance on school partnerships (35% of revenue)
- **Mitigation**: Diversify across 5+ revenue streams, target multiple customer segments, build recurring revenue base

**2. Pricing Pressure**
- **Risk**: Competitors undercutting prices, reducing margins
- **Mitigation**: Premium positioning based on results, strong brand loyalty, focus on value delivery, differentiation

**3. Cost Inflation**
- **Risk**: Rising instructor salaries, facility costs, or operational expenses
- **Mitigation**: Operational efficiency, technology leverage, strategic partnerships, pricing adjustments

**4. Customer Churn**
- **Risk**: Higher than expected customer attrition, reducing lifetime value
- **Mitigation**: Focus on retention, loyalty programs, continuous improvement, customer satisfaction

**5. Funding Gap**
- **Risk**: Insufficient cash to fund growth initiatives
- **Mitigation**: Secure HK$600K funding, reduce marketing if needed, improve cash conversion

**6. Execution Risk**
- **Risk**: Inability to execute on growth plan, missing targets
- **Mitigation**: Experienced management team, clear KPIs, regular monitoring, contingency planning
