## 1. Registration & Class Booking

Swimfit offers swimming classes through our online booking system available at www.swimfit.hk. Class availability is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.

**Returning Students:** Existing participants receive priority booking for subsequent terms, provided they confirm their enrolment by the specified deadline. Places not confirmed by this deadline may become available for new students.

**Priority System:** Group classes are given priority in our scheduling and allocation system compared to private or semi-private instruction.

**Booking Confirmation:** Upon successful registration, an automated confirmation email will be sent to the email address provided. Should you not receive this confirmation within seven days, please reach out to us at swimfit.hk@gmail.com.

## 2. Payment Terms & Billing

### Billing Through Partner Venues

When you enrol through a participating venue partner, your account will be billed monthly in accordance with that venue's standard billing procedures and schedule.

### Direct Payment with Swimfit

Participants enrolling directly with Swimfit are required to settle payment before classes commence.

- Swimfit will issue an invoice via email detailing your programme fees
- Your enrolment is considered active only upon receipt of full payment
- A receipt will be issued for your records—retain this as proof of transaction
- If payment remains outstanding, Swimfit may restrict access to classes and cancel your enrolment
- All outstanding balances remain due regardless of enrolment status

## 3. Absence & Non-Attendance

Absence from scheduled classes—whether due to illness, personal commitments, holidays, or any other reason—does not entitle you to refunds, credits, or alternative sessions. You remain responsible for all fees for classes within your enrolment period, whether attended or not.

## 4. Programme Modifications & Facility Closures

Swimfit may cancel or modify classes due to:

- Severe weather conditions (Typhoon Signal 3 or higher, rainstorm warnings)
- Unsafe swimming conditions or water contamination
- Facility emergencies or maintenance requirements
- Government-mandated closures or public health directives
- Other circumstances beyond Swimfit's reasonable control

**Weather-Related Cancellations:** When Swimfit initiates a cancellation due to adverse weather, we will notify participants via email, SMS, or WhatsApp with at least one hour's advance notice where possible. Cancelled classes will result in either a full refund or credit toward future sessions.

**Outdoor Venue Adjustments:** For outdoor pools, Swimfit may issue a class credit if cancellation occurs prior to the scheduled start time. Once a class has commenced, it is considered complete, and no credits will be issued regardless of subsequent weather deterioration.

**Transport & Other Interruptions:** No refunds or credits will be provided for cancellations related to public transport disruptions, emergencies, or other external circumstances beyond Swimfit's control.

## 5. Private & Semi-Private Lesson Cancellation Policy

Participants wishing to cancel a booked private or semi-private lesson must provide notice as follows:

**Short Notice Cancellations (Less than 24 hours):** The full lesson fee is charged.

**Adequate Notice (24 hours or more):** An administration fee applies based on lesson duration:
- 30-minute lesson: $200
- 45-minute lesson: $300
- 60-minute class: $400

**Term Termination:** To discontinue private lesson arrangements mid-term, provide two weeks' written notice or pay an equivalent termination fee.

**Excessive Cancellations:** Should more than 50% of your scheduled classes be cancelled during a term, Swimfit reserves the right to charge full fees for all remaining scheduled sessions.

**Mid-Term Enrolment:** Participants joining after a term has commenced may be subject to pro-rata pricing based on remaining sessions and availability.

## 6. Group Class Withdrawal

Should you wish to withdraw from a group class programme, one month's written notice (or payment equivalent) is required. 

**Medical Exceptions:** Withdrawal requests supported by medical documentation (serious illness or permanent incapacity) may be processed with shorter notice periods at Swimfit's discretion.
