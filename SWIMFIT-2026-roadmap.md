# SWIMFIT 2026 Roadmap

## Executive Summary

SWIMFIT's 2026 roadmap outlines a comprehensive execution plan to establish market leadership in Hong Kong's premium swimming education sector. The roadmap is organized by quarter, with clear milestones, deliverables, and success metrics aligned to strategic objectives of expanding school partnerships, establishing multi-venue presence, building community, and achieving HK$5.4M revenue with 25% EBITDA margins.

**Strategic Objectives (2026)**:
1. Expand from 4 to 15+ school partnerships
2. Establish presence at 8-10 premium pool venues across Hong Kong
3. Build 50K+ social media community and establish thought leadership
4. Achieve HK$5.4M annual revenue with 25% EBITDA margins
5. Recruit and onboard 4-6 additional coaches
6. Develop technology platform for progress tracking and scheduling
7. Create sustainable, scalable business model for future expansion

- **Mitigation**: Dedicated sales manager, proven curriculum, flexible terms, regular reviews
- **Contingency**: Increase focus on private lessons and corporate programs

**2. Facility Access Risk**
- **Risk**: Loss of access to key pool venues
- **Mitigation**: Diversify across 8-10 venues, long-term agreements, strong relationships
- **Contingency**: Develop relationships with alternative venues, mobile programs

**3. Coaching Quality Risk**
- **Risk**: Difficulty recruiting or retaining qualified coaches
- **Mitigation**: Competitive compensation, professional development, clear career paths
- **Contingency**: Increase private lesson rates, reduce group class sizes, outsource specialized programs

**4. Market Competition Risk**
- **Risk**: Increased competition from established or new players
- **Mitigation**: Premium positioning, strong brand, institutional partnerships, continuous innovation
- **Contingency**: Increase marketing investment, expand service offerings, geographic expansion

**5. Financial Risk**
- **Risk**: Insufficient cash flow or profitability
- **Mitigation**: Diversified revenue streams, cost control, cash management, funding secured
- **Contingency**: Reduce marketing investment, focus on profitability, seek additional funding

**6. Execution Risk**
- **Risk**: Inability to execute on roadmap
- **Mitigation**: Clear KPIs, regular monitoring, experienced team, contingency planning
- **Contingency**: Adjust timelines, reprioritize initiatives, seek external support
