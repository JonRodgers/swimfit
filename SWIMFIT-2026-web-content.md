### Q1: What age can children start swimming lessons?
**A**: We accept children from age 4. Younger children can start with parent-child classes. Every child develops at their own pace, and we customize lessons accordingly.

### Q2: How long does it take to learn to swim?
**A**: Most children develop water confidence and basic skills in 4-8 weeks with consistent lessons. Competitive swimmers continue progressing for years.

### Q3: What if my child is afraid of water?
**A**: We specialize in trauma-informed coaching for anxious swimmers. Many of our success stories involve children who started terrified and now love swimming.

### Q4: Do you offer group or private lessons?
**A**: Both! We offer private lessons, semi-private coaching, and group classes. We'll recommend the best format for your goals.

### Q5: What are your prices?
**A**: Pricing varies by program. Private lessons range from HK$800-1,000. Group classes are HK$400-600/month. Contact us for a personalized quote.

### Q6: Where do you teach?
**A**: We teach at 8-10 premium pool venues across Hong Kong, including Harrow International School and Country Club Hong Lok Yuen.

### Q7: Can adults learn to swim?
**A**: Absolutely! We have many adult students who learned to swim for the first time. It's never too late.

### Q8: Do you offer water polo coaching?
**A**: Yes! We offer mini-polo for kids and competitive water polo coaching for teams.

### Q9: What about open water swimming?
**A**: Our monthly open water swim series is one of Hong Kong's most loved aquatic events. We also offer open water coaching.

### Q10: How do I get started?
